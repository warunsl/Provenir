About this project

Inspiration

Inspiration of this project comes from excessively watching TV shows, particularly White Collar. The protagonist of the show, Neal Caffrey has a special knack at tracing artworks to their origins. So when our professor Pedro suggested Art as one of the themes for the project, Provenir was a no brainer ;)

Technologies and Tools

The website currently works on the provenance records obtained from The Getty Research Institute and The National Gallery of Art. We have extensively used Open Refine to clean the data from the sources. GRefine RDF extension for Open Refine was useful in reconciling the cleaned data against DBpedia. We accomplished record linkage at various stages of the project using FRIL. The website runs as a Python Flask application using MongoDB as a data store. We use bootstrap for the CSS and JS library for the type ahead search.

Team

The intention of the project is to apply the concepts and methodologies studied as a part of the CS548 course at University of Southern California. Varun and Gladon are masters students at the Computer Science department of USC. If you have any queries, feel free to contact us.
